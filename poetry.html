Mahender - Poet and Writer

Books and Contributions

Influenced by the Malidasa Telangana movement, Mahender has authored seven books of poetry and edited four more books under his editorship.

Year 2011

Published and unveiled his first book 'Poruganam' in 2011 to sensitize the Telangana movement.

In the same year, protested the self-sacrifice of the students in the movement and wrote a booklet called 'Balidanala Maruddam' to inspire them, presenting it in various meetings.

Year 2012

In 2012, wrote a book of poetry titled 'Pidikili' (Telangana Poetry) to give life to the movement.

Year 2014

In 2014, compiled a poetry collection titled 'Kaalani Gelutu' (Poetry on Special Talents) under his editorship, instilling self-confidence among the disabled.

Year 2016

In 2016, wrote a multidisciplinary collection 'Konni Prasnalu-Konni Jnapakaalu'' and another poetry collection called 'Gelupu Chirunaama' (Poetry on Special Talents) introducing a different entity in literature.

Year 2020

Wrote two poetry books 'Ippudoka Patakavali' and 'Tanunenu Vayakam' in response to various social issues and injustices.

Edited the volumes of Kovid-19 (Poetry of Orugallu poets on Corona Virus) and Valasa Dukhkham (a collection of poetry on migrant workers during Corona) under his editorship.

Year 2022

Published a collection of poetry called 'Bilkis Bano' in solidarity with Bilkis Bano under his editorship.

Continuously working in literature by bringing collections on contemporary issues.

Other Contributions

Participated in many district and state level poetry gatherings and effectively performed his poetry.

Participated in the 'All India Differently Able Writers Meet' organized by Central Sahitya Akademi in Delhi and recited poetry from Telugu language.

Wrote essays on contemporary topics, reviewed literary books, and organized literary programs to encourage budding poets.

Fought actively in the Telangana movement without considering his disability, participating in Rastarokolu and protest meetings.

United the disabled and made them participate in the movement meetings.

As the general secretary of Warangal Joint District of Telangana Writers Association, serving literature and organizing literary, historical, and cultural meetings.

Won many awards as a poet.
